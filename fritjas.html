Odin Recipes - Freya's Fritjas

  • Grate 2 large potatoes and squeeze out excess moisture
  • Mix with 1 beaten egg, 2 tbsp flour, and salt
  • Form into small patties
  • Fry in hot oil until golden and crispy (about 3-4 minutes per side)
  • Drain on paper towels
  • Serve with a dollop of sour cream mixed with chopped dill
